I Believe

I believe in OpenAI products and leadership. And I believe in myself to do this job.

I've sold full-stack cloud services at AWS for 5+ years and have loved doing it for many reasons, but the biggest being: because I believed in what I was selling. That conviction comes through. Customers feel it. When you genuinely believe in the product, you're not pitching to the customer, you're just having a conversation: about what the customer is missing, what they need, and how your product can help them.

I've followed what OpenAI has been building for years via YouTube videos, LinkedIn/X/blog posts, customer success stories, and have also received feedback firsthand from co-founders in my network who build & ship on OpenAI. I believe in OpenAI, and I believe I can convey with customers as to why they need your tools, (or who need help understanding why they need them).
